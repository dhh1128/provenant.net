Unsolicited calls from questionable sources are rife. We answer scammers or telemarketers during work meetings and family events, and feel victimized.

Regulators expect carriers to fix this. But caller ID is iffy. Maybe the caller's phone number is in a database, but the number can be faked...

Provenant's Origin platform changes this. Every call gets a cryptographically verifiable origin. This doesn't just prove who called; it proves where their business is registered, and what kind of business it is.

Goodbye, robocalls. Hello, authentic digital communication.
